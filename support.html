Common questions

What is SugaMeter?

A daily added-sugar tracker with a visual jar meter and streaks.

How do I log a treat?

Tap a treat, pick Small/Medium/Large or use Custom grams, and it logs instantly.

What's "More Options"?

It opens the Treat Library with all items, search, and category filters.

How do I add a custom treat?

Tap "Add" and enter name, grams, and a category.

Can I remove a custom treat?

Long-press the custom item, then confirm removal.

How is the streak calculated?

It counts consecutive days with any logged sugar; it resets if a day is missed.

When does the daily total reset?

It resets at local midnight.

Can I change the daily target?

Yes, in Settings → Daily Target (36g, 22g, or Custom).

Does the app store my data online?

No, it's stored locally on your device.

Why isn't the widget updating?

Open the app once or tap Settings → Refresh Widget; updates can take a minute or two.

Why does the widget show 0g?

If it's out of sync, open the app, log once, then refresh the widget or re-add it.

Can I edit past logs?

Not yet—only totals are tracked per day.
