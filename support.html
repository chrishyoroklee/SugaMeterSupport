Common questions

How do I export my journal?

Share your log from the app settings. You can save or send it to your care team anytime.

Can I change my target range?

Yes. Open Settings, tap Targets, and slide to the range your care plan recommends.

Is there a reminder for post-meal checks?

Turn on Gentle Nudges in Reminders. We use soft timing so it feels supportive, not pushy.

Reach the care team

We respond within 1-2 business days. Include your device model and app version for faster help.
